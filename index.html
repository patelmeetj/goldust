Universities / colleges

The Goldust Township neighbourhood has top-ranked universities and colleges offering world-class education and opportunities for personal and career growth. Residents have access to diverse academic programs, hands-on learning, and research. The neighbourhood provides a valuable environment for education and career advancement.

Shopping Mall

The neighbourhood boasts a modern shopping mall offering a diverse range of shopping, dining, and entertainment options for residents. Enjoy shopping for fashion, specialty items, and more, followed by a meal or entertainment with friends and family.

Hospital

There is convenient access to quality healthcare services with its state-of-the-art hospital. The hospital provides medical services, including preventative care, diagnostics, and specialized treatments, with highly trained healthcare professionals and cutting-edge technology. Residents can trust the hospital for their healthcare needs.

Airport

The neighbourhood offers easy access to air travel with its proximity to an airport. Residents can easily catch domestic and international flights and enjoy a seamless travel experience. The airport is also surrounded by shopping, dining, and entertainment options.

Railway Station

Goldust Township's proximity to a major railway station offers residents convenient access to rail travel. The railway station provides a variety of trains and modern facilities, making commuting and exploring new places stress-free.

Parking Space

Goldust Township offers ample and secure car parking for its residents, providing peace of mind for vehicle owners. With designated parking spaces for residents and visitors, you can park your car with ease and convenience.

Swimming Pool

We provide a luxurious swimming pool, offering residents the perfect place to beat the heat, relax, and enjoy quality time with family and friends. With clear, refreshing water and ample sunbathing space, the swimming pool is a haven for rejuvenation and recreation.

Private Security

The residents can rest assured knowing that their safety is a top priority. The community is equipped with a private security force, providing 24/7 protection and peace of mind. With vigilant monitoring and quick response times, residents can live with confidence knowing that their homes and loved ones are secure.

Medical Center

Goldust Township provides residents with convenient access to top-notch medical care with its on-site medical center. Boasting a team of experienced healthcare professionals and state-of-the-art facilities, the medical center offers comprehensive medical services for all your healthcare needs, ensuring that you always have access to quality medical care when you need it.

Library Area

The Township offers a tranquil and inspiring environment for reading and learning with its on-site library. With a diverse collection of books, magazines, and other reading materials, the library allows residents to expand their knowledge, escape into a new world, and relax with a good book. Whether you're looking to learn something new or just want to curl up with a good book, the library has something for everyone.

Smart Homes

Experience the future of living with the smart homes at Goldust Township. With cutting-edge technology integrated into every aspect of your home, you'll enjoy unparalleled comfort, convenience, and security. From controlling your lights, temperature, and appliances with your voice or smartphone, to having the latest in-home security features, smart homes at Goldust Township offer a unique and modern living experience like no other.

Kid’s Playland

Our kid’s playland is a safe and exciting place for children to play, learn, and grow. With equipment and a variety of activities, kids can enjoy hours of fun and adventure. From slides to swings, and everything in between, the playland provides a nurturing and stimulating environment for children to develop important skills and make new friends.

Underground Cabling

The underground cabling system ensures that your home is equipped with the latest technology and is protected from power outages. With a seamless, hidden network of wiring and cables, residents can enjoy uninterrupted services, improved safety, and a more attractive community appearance.
