Overview

The concept of Goldust Township is based on the idea of one gesture, one design, one identity. The project has been crafted keeping in mind the needs of the city dwellers and also those who are commuting in and out of Ludhiana on a daily basis. As every individual has unique requirements, Goldust Township is aimed at fulfilling whatever people’s heart desire in a convenient and upscale manner, elevating the standards of living of the people of Ludhiana. With an Eclectic Product Mix, Goldust Township will offer the most relaxed contemporary lifestyle that people never indulged in before.

  • Location: Ludhiana
  • Type: Commercial
  • Offerings: Showrooms, Food Court
